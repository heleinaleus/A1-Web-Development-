Princess Mononoke

"Life is suffering. It is hard. The world is cursed. But still, you find reasons to keep living."

Overview

On a journey to find the cure for a Tatarigami's curse, Ashitaka finds himself in the middle of a war between the forest gods and Tatara, a mining colony. In this quest he also meets San, the Mononoke Hime.

Characters

  • Ashitaka - a prince from an extinct tribe of people known as the Emishi clan.
  • San - a princess raised by wolves.
  • Lady Eboshi - The head of Irontown and the mortal enemy of the dying forest.
  • Moro - the wolf god, the mother of San and the chief protector of the forest.
  • Jigo - a priest that Ashitaka meets early on in his journey.
  • Shishigami - the giver and taker of life in the forest.
